Our Founding

Established in 2015, We have provided our clients in the healthcare sector across the region with intelligent, scalable clinic management and electronic medical record systems.

Cloud integration

UNO went with cloud service from the start. We are one of the first few cloud-based health-tech solutions provider in Singapore. A true pioneer in that aspect. Helping medical practitioners going paperless and improve their data management.

IHiS SmartCMS integration

In 2018, UNO successfully integrated with the Ministry of Health Singapore IHiS SmartCMS programme. Becoming a preferred and certified SmartCMS vendor, making UNO an NEHR ready CMS provider.

A new beginning

In late 2019, UNO Technologies was acquired by Essex Bio-Technology Limited. We went through a rebranding and became a part of the UNO Medical Group, a group dedicated to providing healthcare services and health-tech solutions to the APAC region. UNO Technologies is a fully owned subsidiary of Essex-Bio-Technology Limited.
